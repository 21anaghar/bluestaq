Assumptions:

1. Floors are numbered from 1 to Maxfloor.
2. The elevator starts at floor 1 and there is one elevator
3. If multiple requests are in up and down directions, the elevator will process all requests in one direction before switching.
4. While the elevator is processing requests, no additional requests are queued

Features Not Implemented:
1. Does not handle requests simultaneously
2. No emergency stops
3. While the elevator is processing requests, no additional requests are queued
